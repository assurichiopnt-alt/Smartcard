The card

One card, two sides

FRONT

Sales contact side

Name, title, phone and email — the printed face people keep. Looks like a premium metal business card.

BACK

Data link side

An embedded NFC chip. Tap it to a phone and it opens your lead-capture link — no app, no typing a URL.

How it works

From tap to Data Hub

Click the button to advance the flow one step at a time and watch the customer's screen.

1

Tap the card to a phone

Hold the chip side to the back of the customer's phone — no app required.

2

The link opens itself

The phone pops your capture link, pre-tagged with the rep who was tapped.

3

Customer fills the form

Name, phone, email and interest — a short form built to complete fast.

4

Stored in the Data Hub

The lead lands in your central hub instantly, linked to rep and product.
